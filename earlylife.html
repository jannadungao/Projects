Taylor Swift's Early Life

Taylor Swift was born to Andrea Finlay and Scott Kingsley Swift in West Reading, Pennsylvania, on December 13, 1989. She began music at an early age and by 11 she was singing "The Star-Spangled Banner" at a 76ers basketball game. By 14, she had signed with Sony/ATV and moved to Nashville, Tennessee where she faked a country accent (hihi). Since then, she has obviously been a huge success winning hundreds of awards and being instantly recognizable by all.
